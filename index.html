Lives Transformed Through Opportunity

Since its launch, the T.E.A.M.S. Initiative in parnership with thw musk foundations has selected thousands of participants from diverse backgrounds to join its global mentorship network.

Each participant is chosen based on their potential to positively impact their family, profession, and community while inspiring others through service and leadership.

The initiative believes that opportunity should never be limited by age, profession, or background.
